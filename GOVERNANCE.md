# Governance

Small project, honest structure.

## Current state

**Single maintainer:** [@Nandansai08](https://github.com/Nandansai08). All PRs are reviewed and merged by the maintainer; decisions that don't reach consensus in discussion default to the maintainer's call.

## How decisions get made

- **Skill additions/changes:** the [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) anatomy spec and pre-flight checklist are the bar. PRs meeting the bar merge; PRs near the bar get concrete revision feedback.
- **Structural changes** (new categories, anatomy changes, reorganizations): open an issue labeled `discussion` first. The anatomy itself changes rarely and deliberately — it's the product's spine.
- **Disputes about a skill's guidance:** evidence wins. A workflow claim challenged with better evidence (data, incident reports, authoritative sources) gets revised; taste disagreements default to the status quo.

## Becoming a maintainer

Demonstrated through contribution: several merged skills or substantive reviews that hold the quality bar, sustained over months, plus judgment in discussions. When that pattern exists, the current maintainer extends commit rights and updates this file. No formal application — the track record is the application.

## If the maintainer disappears

The MIT license makes forking legitimate and expected. A community fork that maintains the quality bar is a success outcome, not a hostile one.
